Also of note are my statistical skills which have been honed over three years of undergraduate laboratories and in my MSc course, Data Analysis Techniques. I am also well versed in Solid State physics and circuitry having taken both standard and advanced Condensed State Physics. The more theoretical subjects have little direct applications to practical systems but do show my ability to learn difficult concepts in a short amount of time.
For Quantum Field theory and Group theory, a high mathematical ability is required. QFT is based in our 4 dimensional world and requires the extensive use of tensors, and Group theory makes use of Lie Algebras. Supersymmetry also requires its own notion in the form of Superspace and Superfields. On top of more theoretical maths I have also studied statistics extensively, having done a masters level course in it.
