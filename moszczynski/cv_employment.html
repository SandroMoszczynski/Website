    Key modules: Quantum Communication, Machine Learning with Big Data, Research with C++ and Python.

    My Quantum Technologies Masters focused on two main areas. Programming and Quantum Computing. For the former I took modules that taught me valuable Python and C++ principles and techniques, as well as giving me knowledge of machine learning algorithms. For the Quantum Computing I took modules which dealt mostly with the theoretical side of the field. Although some practical applications were explored in my project.
